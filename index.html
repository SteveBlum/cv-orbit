Career Profile

I am a Cloud Software Architect with over 10 years of experience in IT consulting and software development. Based on this experience, I ultimately decided to switch tracks and join the Solution Development team 6 years ago. Since then, I've established and led a Cloud solution development team that I've grown to over 25 professionals specializing in DevOps, frontend, and backend development. Throughout various software development projects, I've fulfilled multiple leadership roles including Stream Lead, Solution Architect, Project Lead, and Product Owner.
